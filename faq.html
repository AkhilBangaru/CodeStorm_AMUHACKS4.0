Frequently Asked Questions

What is ProFitness?

ProFitness is a comprehensive fitness app that helps you track workouts, set goals, follow healthy recipes, and manage your fitness journey.

How do I create a workout plan?

You can create a workout plan by visiting the Workout Planner page, filling out the details of your workout, and saving it to your profile.

Can I track my mental health progress?

Yes! ProFitness provides tips and advice for maintaining good mental health, which is essential for your overall fitness journey.

How do I set fitness goals?

You can set your fitness goals by going to the Goals & Challenges section, where you can define your goals and set deadlines for achieving them.

What kind of recipes are available?

ProFitness offers a variety of healthy recipes that are designed to fuel your body and enhance your fitness journey. Recipes include high-protein meals, healthy snacks, and more!
